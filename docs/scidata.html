Scientific Data

Datasets, supplementary materials, and data products associated with published research.

Available Datasets

Hunga Tonga–Hunga Ha'apai Eruption: Supplementary Data & Movies

Supplementary data files and movies for the eruption chronology of the December 2021 to January 2022 Hunga Tonga–Hunga Ha'apai eruption sequence, including Himawari-8 brightness temperature fields, umbrella-cloud masks, and radial expansion time series.
Repository: Zenodo (DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.7250365)
Associated publication: Gupta et al. (2022), Communications Earth & Environment.

21st-Century Global Dust Cycle Reconstruction

Monthly, size-resolved 4-D fields of dust aerosol optical depth (DAOD), dust loading, concentrations, and emission/deposition fluxes (2003–2023) derived from large-ensemble Monte Carlo inverse modeling constrained by multi-sensor satellite records (IASI, MIDAS, CALIPSO), three aerosol reanalyses, and AERONET sites.
Status: Dataset to be released upon publication in Science Advances (2026).
Associated preprint: Research Square.

Hunga Eruption Radiative Forcing Data

Stratospheric composition observations and idealized radiative transfer model simulation outputs used to estimate the net top-of-atmosphere radiative energy loss from the 2022 Hunga eruption (sulfate aerosols, water vapor, and ozone perturbations).
Associated publication: Gupta et al. (2025), Communications Earth & Environment. DOI
